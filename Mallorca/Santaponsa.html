About Santa Ponsa

Also known by the spanish as the irish Domain.

At first glance Santa Ponsa, or Santa Ponça seems rather a town suitable for family vacations; however, it hosts all kinds of natural and historical attractions, as well as some of the most luxurious properties in Mallorca . The town center has stunning views of the mountains and forests, while in the surroundings you will find a golf course and various houses and estates of the yacht owners of nearby Port Adriano.

Located in the southwest of Mallorca, Santa Ponsa has two sandy beaches: a large beach of 560 meters long and a somewhat smaller cove called Caló d'en Pallisser, locally known as' the beach small0. In Santa Ponsa you will find everything you need to spend a typical holiday with family and by the sea.

With my guide you'll get to know everything you need to know about Santa Ponsa to get the most out of your visit to this locality and its surroundings.

History and Culture of Santa Ponsa

Santa Ponsa is well known in Mallorcan history: Sa Caleta, which now houses the Santa Ponsa Marina, is the site where King Jaume I settled on 10 September 1229 to begin the reconquest of the island and defeat the Moors . This historic site is now marked with a large stone cross and the events are commemorated each year in a the King's Jaume festival. There are also several sites with archaeological legacy dating from the Roman and Roman occupation era.

The city has a mix of international residents and much of the architectural style is geared towards tourism. In Santa Ponsa itself you will see many families enjoying their holidays by the sea, but Santa Ponsa is also an area where you will find all kinds of services, as in this area are some of the most luxurious properties of the island.

Nova Santa Ponsa is known for its golf courses and in the surroundings you will find all kinds of protected green areas with spectacular views, such as the supposedly magical Galatzó mountain.

You will find all kinds of shops for shopping, as well as the typical weekly market that takes place on Saturdays. Nearby is the Son Bugadelles Polygon , an industrial estate where you will find a large gym and all kinds of shops that sell wholesale and retail, including several designer stores, such as Knox Design .
